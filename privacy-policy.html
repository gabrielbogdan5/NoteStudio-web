Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025
This privacy policy describes how the NoteStudio app collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the app, you agree to the terms described below.
01

Data We Collect

NoteStudio is a local notes application. Your notes are stored exclusively on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.

  • Note content (title, text) — stored locally on your device
  • App preferences (theme, language, font size) — stored locally
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
02

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of the app contains ads provided by Google AdMob. By displaying ads, Google may collect certain data according to its own privacy policy.

What AdMob may collect:
Device advertising identifiers (GAID), approximate IP address, device type and operating system, interactions with ads. This data is used to display relevant advertisements.

You can manage your advertising preferences in Android Settings → Google → Ads.

Google AdMob Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

03

Permissions Requested

The app requests a minimal set of permissions:

  • Internet — required exclusively for displaying AdMob ads
  • Network access — to check internet connectivity

The app does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or external storage.

04

Data Security

Since all app data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on the security of your device. We recommend using a PIN or fingerprint lock screen.

We do not have access to your notes and cannot recover them if you uninstall the app or clear the app data.

05

Children and Minimum Age

NoteStudio is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us.

06

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. Any changes will be communicated by updating the date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance.
